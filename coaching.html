Coaching

Finding Clarity and Moving Forward

Sometimes life feels stuck — whether it's uncertainty about your next steps, low confidence, or feeling overwhelmed by change. Coaching offers a practical, supportive space to reflect, refocus, and take clear, manageable steps toward what matters most to you.

Why Coaching with Me?

Before becoming a psychotherapist, I spent many years in leadership, coaching, and mentoring roles across various industries. I hold formal qualifications in coaching and mentoring, which complement my practical experience. I’ve also had the privilege of mentoring with Mind, supporting individuals navigating mental health challenges.

My coaching approach blends that experience with empathy and deep listening. I understand the blocks that can show up — self-doubt, career fatigue, motivation struggles — and I’m here to help you explore what’s holding you back and find your own way forward.

What Coaching Can Help With

  • Feeling stuck or unsure about your direction
  • Navigating life transitions and changes
  • Career reflection or dissatisfaction
  • Building confidence and motivation
  • Exploring men’s mental health and identity
  • Reconnecting with what really matters to you

How We Work

Coaching with me is practical and person-centred. It’s not about having all the answers — it’s about discovering yours. We’ll work together at your pace, with honest conversations, gentle challenges when needed, and clear actions to help you move forward.

Sessions are held online via Google Meet and follow the same pricing as counselling:

  • £60 per session
  • £25 concessionary rate (subject to availability)

Next Steps

If this feels like what you’re looking for, I’d love to hear from you. You can reach out via email or the contact form to arrange a free, no-pressure chat.

And if I’m not the right fit, that’s okay — I’m happy to signpost you to someone who might be.
