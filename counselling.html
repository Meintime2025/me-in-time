Counselling Services

Life can throw us curveballs—grief, anxiety, difficult transitions, or the silent struggles many men face but rarely speak about. At Me In Time, I offer integrative counselling tailored just for you. Whether you prefer to meet in person or online, I provide a warm, safe space where your story is honoured, and you can explore it at your own pace.

What I Offer

One-to-One Counselling

Weekly 50-minute sessions, available in person or online, designed to support you wherever you are on your journey.

Individual Work

Individual counselling is often a good starting place for those new to psychotherapy. In a confidential setting, you can speak openly about the distress or disorientation you’re experiencing. I listen attentively and respond sensitively, helping you to understand your current challenges within the broader context of your life and history.

One-to-one work can be an end in itself or a stepping stone towards joining a psychotherapy group, depending on what feels right for you.

Understanding Counselling and Psychotherapy

While counselling and psychotherapy share many similarities, psychotherapy often delves deeper into the roots of psychological difficulties, working with underlying patterns and longer-term change. Counselling typically focuses more on immediate concerns and practical support. My approach is integrative, meaning I tailor what’s best for you — whether that’s counselling, psychotherapy, or a blend of both.

Areas of Focus

Grief and loss, men’s mental health, trauma, life transitions, relationships, self-esteem, and more. I understand how men often carry emotional burdens quietly, sometimes feeling pressure to “keep it together.” My approach creates room to break that silence with compassion and understanding.

Flexible Pricing

Sessions cost £60, with a concessionary rate of £25 available if you need it. I’m happy to discuss what works best for you.

Confidentiality & Ethics

Your privacy matters. All sessions are confidential and follow the ethical guidelines of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP). Personal information is stored securely, fully compliant with GDPR.

How We Work Together

I combine honesty, empathy, and practical support, offering a non-judgmental space to help you find clarity and move forward meaningfully. I know how difficult it can be to ask for help, especially for men, so I’m here to meet you exactly where you are — with patience, respect, and genuine care.
