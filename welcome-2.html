Is Therapy for You?

Thanks for being here. I know reaching out for support isn’t always easy — especially if you're used to carrying a lot, quietly. Whether you're feeling overwhelmed, stuck, grieving, or just unsure of your next step, this is a space where all of that is welcome.

Therapy isn’t about having all the answers — it’s about giving yourself permission to ask the questions. You don’t need to have everything figured out. Just a willingness to be met where you are.

Holding It All Together

Sometimes we all feel like we have to be a superhero. Keep going. Stay strong. Push through. You might be the one others rely on — the quiet one, the capable one, the one who doesn’t complain.

But you don’t have to hold everything on your own. Here, you can let go. Breathe. Be human — not heroic. This space is yours.

A Safe Place to Bring It All Together

Me In Time is a grounded counselling and coaching practice. I offer a space that’s compassionate and honest — where there’s no pressure to “fix” anything, just a chance to explore what’s going on and what might help.

Whether it’s grief, change, anxiety, or just the need to talk without being judged, we can work through it — together, at your pace. You don’t have to go it alone.

Why "Me In Time"?

The name comes from a simple idea: that healing and growth happen when we’re met as we are — not who we think we should be. Me In Time is about meeting yourself with honesty and kindness, even in the messy, uncertain moments.

It’s a space where strength doesn’t have to mean silence. Where you don’t have to keep carrying it alone. If you’d like to know more or arrange a first chat, I’d be happy to hear from you.
