Counselling & Emotional Support for Life’s Toughest Moments
Me In Time offers one-to-one counselling, psychoeducation, and a space for real, human conversations — with a focus on grief, loss, and men’s mental health.

Not sure what’s right for you?

Sometimes life throws challenges at us, and not everyone needs counselling or psychotherapy to work through them. Especially when it’s about work, goals, or feeling stuck, coaching can often be a better fit.

Both counselling and coaching help you feel stronger and more confident, but they do it in different ways.

Think of counselling as a gentle space where you can unpack your emotions, heal old wounds, and better understand yourself — especially when life feels heavy or complicated.

Coaching, on the other hand, is more like having a supportive guide who helps you figure out what you want next, build your confidence, and take practical steps toward your goals.

If you’re wondering which might suit you, feel free to check out my Counselling and Coaching pages, or just get in touch for a friendly, no-pressure chat.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Your privacy matters.

All sessions are confidential and handled in line with the ethical guidelines of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP).

As a practitioner, I am committed to protecting your personal information and complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). I am registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) and adhere to their data protection standards.

How your information is used:

  • Contact information submitted via the website is used solely to respond to your enquiry.
  • No information is shared with third parties.
  • Messages are stored securely and deleted when no longer needed.

If you would like to know more about how your information is handled, you're very welcome to get in touch.
