Coaching & Personal Development

Sometimes what you need isn’t therapy, but a focused, practical space to reflect, refocus, and move forward with clarity. Coaching with Me In Time is about helping you uncover your own answers, build confidence, and reconnect with what truly matters.

What Coaching Can Help With

  • Feeling stuck or uncertain about your next steps
  • Navigating career or life transitions
  • Building self-confidence and motivation
  • Exploring identity, values, and purpose
  • Supporting men’s mental health and wellbeing

How Coaching Works

Sessions last 50 minutes and are held online via Google Meet. In-person sessions are available on request. Together, we’ll create a supportive and honest space for reflection and action. Pricing matches counselling: £50 standard, £25 concessionary.

Confidentiality & Ethics

Coaching conversations are treated with respect and discretion. Although coaching differs from therapy, your privacy and trust are safeguarded at every step.
