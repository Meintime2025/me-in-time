Privacy & Confidentiality

Your privacy is very important to me.

All sessions and communications are confidential, managed according to the ethical guidelines of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP).

As a registered practitioner, I comply fully with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the standards of the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

How your information is handled:

  • Contact details you provide are used only to respond to your enquiry.
  • Your information is never shared with third parties.
  • Messages are securely stored and deleted when no longer needed.

If you want to know more about how your data is protected, please don’t hesitate to get in touch.
