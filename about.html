Thanks for dropping by

Whether you’re here because life feels overwhelming, you’re carrying more than you can manage alone, or you’re simply curious about counselling and coaching — you’ve found a space to pause, breathe, and be truly seen.

I’m Stuart Walker, and Me In Time is a calm, grounded place where real conversations happen, without rush or judgment. Here, you’re invited to be exactly as you are, to explore what’s on your mind, and to find support that’s honest and caring.

Take your time. Look around. When you’re ready, I’m here to listen.
