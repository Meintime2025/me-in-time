Welcome

I'm Stuart Walker, a counsellor offering in-person and online therapy for adults navigating life’s difficult bits — grief, stress, transitions, or just feeling stuck. My approach is warm, grounded, and human. Therapy with me is a space to reflect, reconnect, and move forward with clarity.

How I Work

I offer integrative, person-centred counselling. Sessions are 50 minutes, held weekly either in person (Manchester M21) or online. We start with a free 30-minute consultation to get a sense of whether we’re a good fit.

What I Offer

  • Individual Counselling
  • Career Coaching
  • Psychoeducation Sessions
  • Ongoing Support for Returning Clients

Free Consultation

A 30-minute virtual consultation to talk, ask questions, and see if we’re a good fit. No pressure, no obligation — just space to start the conversation.
