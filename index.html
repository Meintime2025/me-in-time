Welcome to Me In Time

Thank you so much for dropping by.

Me In Time is a space for counselling, reflection, and real human connection. Whether you’re feeling stuck, navigating loss, or seeking support for your mental health — especially men and dads — you’re warmly welcomed here.

I’m Stuart Walker, and my approach is grounded, warm, and compassionate. Together, we’ll create a space where you can be heard without judgment, explore what’s on your mind, and find clarity at your own pace.

If you’re ready to take the first step towards support and growth, please explore the site, read the blog, or get in touch. I’d love to hear from you when the time feels right.
