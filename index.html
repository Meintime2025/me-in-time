Welcome to Me In Time

Counselling & Psychotherapy in Manchester & Online

Maybe you're feeling stuck. Maybe you’re holding more than you can manage on your own. Maybe something's shifted — or maybe nothing has, and that’s part of the problem.

Whatever brings you here, Me In Time is a space to pause and take stock.

I'm Stuart Walker — a therapist who offers time, space, and presence for people navigating the difficult bits of life. My approach is warm, grounded, and human. I don’t offer quick fixes, and I won’t pretend to have all the answers. But I’ll meet you with honesty, care, and curiosity.

I believe therapy is a conversation. It’s a place to explore things you might not say anywhere else. To be heard — without being judged or rushed. To remember your voice. To make space for whatever you’ve been carrying.

This work isn’t about getting it right. It’s about giving yourself time — to make sense, to feel, to breathe, to be.

No need to be polished.
No need to be strong.
No need to be sorry.

Just begin.
