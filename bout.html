About Me

Qualified integrative psychotherapist with a special interest in suicide postvention, bereavement support, and men’s mental health. I support people through grief and loss, with a particular focus on helping men and dads find space to express, reflect, and be truly heard. My trauma-informed, person-centred approach aims to reduce stigma and make meaningful support accessible to all.

I offer 1-2-1 counselling, psychoeducation, and online integrative therapy focused on grief, men’s mental health, and men’s domestic abuse. My standard rate is £50, with a concessionary rate of £25 available and open to discussion.

The reason I’m drawn to work with men is partly due to the closure of Dads Unlimited, a vital service supporting men through separation, emotional struggles, and loneliness. Supporting men’s mental health is more important than ever, and I’m committed to providing compassionate spaces for that work.

Privacy, Ethics & Professional Standards

I work in line with the ethical standards of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP), and I’m registered with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO), complying with UK GDPR guidelines.

All sessions are confidential, and any personal data you share is handled securely.

I’m also professionally insured through Holistic Insurance Services.
